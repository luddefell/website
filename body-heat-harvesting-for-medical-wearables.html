ZT Group Research - Thermoelectric Generator Development

Undergraduate research project focused on developing thermoelectric generator circuits that harvest body heat to power wearable medical devices. Worked with organic thermoelectric device prototypes to characterize power output across variable thermal gradients.

research components

  • PCB layouts for TEG measurement and data logging in Altium.
  • Thermoelectric characterization across variable thermal gradients.
  • Power electronics for energy harvesting in wearable medical devices.
